Plan, Experience, Reminisce

Waypoint is a self-hosted travel planner, logger, and tracker. Your trips, your itineraries, your memories — on your own server.

Get started View on GitHub 
docker pull ghcr.io/ctrl-research/waypoint:latest

Everything a trip needs

One app for the whole arc of travel — dreaming, planning together, being there, and looking back.

🗺️

Trips on a real map

Areas — countries, cities, or regions, whatever fits the trip — and every itinerary item pinned at its actual venue. Raster or vector tiles, your choice of provider.

🧑‍🤝‍🧑

Plan together on layers

Everyone keeps their own idea layers next to the shared Main layer. The itinerary is simply the merge of what's visible — toggle a layer's eye and the whole plan updates.

▶️

Trip replay

Watch the journey play out: the camera rides a dot through every stop, flights arc across the map with a ✈️ in tow, and a scrubber lets you seek, pause, and change speed.

📖

Journal the days

Day-by-day notes in markdown with photo uploads, woven into the trip timeline alongside the itinerary.

📅

Calendar & ICS sync

All trips on a month grid, per-trip .ics export, and a private subscription feed that keeps Google or Apple Calendar up to date as plans change.

📊

Travel stats

Countries, cities, and continents — travelled in green, still-planned as an amber +N — with a visited-world map, flight and train tallies, and trips per year.

🤖

Bring your own AI

An optional MCP endpoint lets any LLM client manage trips with a personal token — paste old travel notes into Claude and let it backfill years of history.

🔗

Share & export

Read-only share links for friends, plus GPX, GeoJSON, Markdown, ICS, and print-to-PDF exports. Your data is never locked in.

🔐

Self-hosted & private

A single Go binary with the UI embedded, Postgres behind it, Google sign-in with an email allowlist, and multi-arch images published on every release.

Up and running in a minute

Grab the compose file, set a password, and go. A guided tour with demo data greets the first sign-in.

git clone https://github.com/ctrl-research/waypoint
cd waypoint
cp .env.example .env
docker compose up -d        # app on :8080
